GearType
========

GearType is an enum that maps type of gear pieces to a unique id.

**Enum**

* WEAPON = 0
* SHIELD = 1 
* HEAD = 2
* BODY = 3
* HANDS = 4
* LEGS = 5
* FEET = 6
* EARRINGS = 7
* NECKLACE = 8
* BRACELETS = 9
* LRING = 10 (Left ring)
* RING = 11 (Right ring)
