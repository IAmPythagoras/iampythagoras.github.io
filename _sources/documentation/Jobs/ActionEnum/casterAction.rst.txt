CasterAction 
============

This is a subclass of ActionEnum. CasterAction is an enum mapping the name of actions
that casters can do to their unique id.

**Enum**

* Swiftcast = 7561
* LucidDreaming = 7562
* Surecast = 7559
* Addle = 7560
* Sleep = 25880
* Potion = -2
* WaitAbility = 212
* CasterLB1 = 1111
* CasterLB2 = 1112
* CasterLB3 = 1113
