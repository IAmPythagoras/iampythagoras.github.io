RangedAction 
============

This is a subclass of ActionEnum. RangedAction is an enum mapping the name of actions
that ranged can do to their unique id.

**Enum**

* LegGraze = 7554
* FootGraze = 7553
* HeadGraze = 7551
* Peloton = 7557
* SecondWind = 7541
* ArmLength = 7548
* Potion = -2
* WaitAbility = 212
* AA = -23
* RangedLB1 = 1111
* RangedLB2 = 1112
* RangedLB3 = 1113
